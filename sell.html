Straightforward review

Plain conversation, item in hand.

Most selling questions get easier once the item is on the counter. The staff can see what is there, note the condition, and talk plainly about what the shop can do.

That keeps the process practical for firearms, silver, jewelry, tools, lawn equipment, air conditioners, electronics, and other common local items without turning it into a long back-and-forth.
