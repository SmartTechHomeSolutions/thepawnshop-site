Core services

Choose the conversation that fits your visit.

Each service is handled in person, with the item in front of the staff and the current store selection close by.

Pawn

Use an item of value for a pawn loan.

Bring the item in, let staff review it, and talk through the loan option face to face.

Buy

Sell something directly to the shop.

For customers who want a straight sale, the conversation starts with an in-store review.

Sell

Browse what is available on the floor.

Look through the current selection and ask staff about what is on hand that day.

Trade

Compare your item against store inventory.

Bring in what you have and see whether a trade makes more sense than buying outright.
